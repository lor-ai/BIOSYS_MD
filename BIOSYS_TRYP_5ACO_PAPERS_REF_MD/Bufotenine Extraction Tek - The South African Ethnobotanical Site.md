Bufotenine Extraction Tek

A good extraction tek is essential if you want to truly experience the awesomeness of Bufotenine. This is where most people fail and they assume that

Bufotenine is simply not worth the effort. It is more than worth it if you are prepared to put in a little effort. I have researched many extraction teks. I
even tried a few of them. A lot of them seemed flaky and I read very contradictory reports on some of it. Many of them apparently don't work at all and

seems to be a waste of time. Others do work but requires a lot of chemicals. I have decided on this one because it uses easy to find chemicals are it is
very easy to do. I was very impressed with the result. You can have relatively pure bufo in a just few hours.

Here’s what’s needed:

* Acetone: available at most hardware stores. It’s best to use distilled acetone that you distilled yourself if you’re at all worried about contaminants.
KleanStrip acetone is pure acetone and 100.0 % volatile according to their MSDS, so there will be no residue left behind after evaporating it. SWIM uses

this brand, always distils it, and never finds any left over residue. It seems to be very clean.

* Sodium Carbonate: The original tek calls for Pickling lime (Calcium Hydroxide). I decided not to use it because it often destroys Bufotenine. If you
cannot find Sodium Carbonate you can make your own by baking Sodium Bi-Carbonate in the oven at 300F for 2.5 hours. Just spread it out evenly in a

shallow oven dish and bake it. It will convert to Sodium Carbonate. Store it in an airtight container. It is hygroscopic and will draw moisture from the air
and convert back to bi-carbonate.

* Citric acid: available as “sour salt” in some grocery stores. It’s often used in canning. It’s available from many health food or vitamin shops. This is

found naturally in fruit.

* Filtering system: coffee filters aren’t that good but will work. For best filtering results it’s better to use a lab filtering system (glass micro-fibre filters, a
vacuum pump, and a filtering flask, Buchner funnel, etc.) I just used coffee filters and it worked fine.

Here's the extraction steps:

1) Prepare citric acid solution by adding 1 gram of citric acid to 10 ml of acetone. We’ll use it later so put this aside, go onto step 2, but mix the citric

acid solution occasionally. It will eventually all dissolve after about an hour or so.

2) Mix 100 grams of crushed Anadenanthera seeds (either peregrina or colubrina works) with and equal volume of Sodium Carbonate and add enough
water to form a paste. Let it sit for about 1 hour, mixing it a few times. Dry it completely in an oven at 150 F.

3) Break apart the dried material. Add 500 ml of acetone. Mix for about 5 minutes. Let the dry material settle to the bottom. The acetone should be

reddish amber in color. Pour the acetone through a good quality filter. I found 500 ml is not enough and added it a little extra.

4) Add 10 ml of citric acid solution (from step 1) to the filtered acetone and mix. I was amazed. The Bufotenine crashed immediately. it looked like
yellow mustard crashing out of the solution as I started adding the Citric acid solution. Let the crystals settle to the bottom. Poor off the acetone, being

careful not to pour off the crystals. Discard the acetone. Wash the crystals by adding 100 ml of fresh acetone, mix, let the crystals settle, and then
carefully pour off the acetone and discard it (EDIT: you might want to let the acetone evaporate down to about 50 ml before discarding, as more

precipitates might form. Keep in mind that a small amount of the alkaloids won’t precipitate out of the acetone. A small portion of this contains the tiny
amount of DMT present in the seeds. If you save the acetone, you could extract the last bit of alkaloids using an A/B extraction, but It’s not really worth

the trouble). Let all the acetone evaporate off the crystals. They’ll start absorbing water from the air and become dark brown goo once all the acetone
has evaporated.

5) Mix the crystal goo with an equal volume of Sodium Carbonate. Add enough water to form a thin watery paste. Let it sit for about 1 hour while

mixing a few times. This will again freebase the Bufotenine. Dry in an oven at 150 F.

6) Break apart the dried material. Dissolve in 100 ml acetone for about 5 minutes, mixing a few times. Filter off the pickling time. Evaporate the acetone
leaving behind 5-HO-DMT freebase as a single solid mass of amber micro-crystals (and a few other active freebase alkaloids). It looks like amber candy,

not crystals. It’s very difficult to form large crystals, so don’t bother. Just scrape it up and store in an air tight container away from light.
