Step 3

If this has been successful, a clear and separate layer of d-Limonene should be visible at

the top of the jars liquid content. A turkey baster is then used to collect the d-Limonene and

put into the 200ml jar. The d-Limonene should be quite orange in color, as d-Limonene is a

full range extraction.

No brown liquid should be tolerated as this affects the DMT.

If the d-Limonene has a tint of brown orange, leave the d-Limonene to sit in the separate jar

overnight, the brown liquid should then separate and should be removed before moving on

to the next step.

Step 4

50ml of Vinegar should then be poured into the jar of d-Limonene and shaken vigorously

until a deep orange color accumulates within the vinegar, the vinegar will separate and

collect in the bottom as it converts the DMT from salt and into an acetate.

Step 5

Once the vinegar has sufficiently absorbed the DMT, pour off the d-Limonene and repeat

step 4 until the vinegar stays clear. You should then pour all the collective vinegar into a

large dish and allow it to evaporate. Placing the dish in front of a fan will speed things up

considerably.
